Our Story

Founded in 2015, Gilcakes Events has established itself as Uganda's most sought-after luxury cake design studio. Our journey began with a simple passion for transforming ordinary ingredients into extraordinary edible art.

Today, we're recognized for our meticulous attention to detail, innovative designs, and commitment to using only the finest ingredients. Each creation tells a story, reflecting our dedication to craftsmanship and artistic excellence.

Our team of award-winning designers and pastry chefs work closely with clients to bring their visions to life, whether it's an intimate celebration or a grand gala event.
